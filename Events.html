Events and Festivals

Afghanistan has a rich culture and heritage that is reflected in its numerous festivals and events. Some of the most popular festivals in Afghanistan include Eid al-Fitr, which marks the end of Ramadan, Nowruz, the Afghan New Year, and Janda bala, a kite-flying festival celebrated in Kabul. These festivals are celebrated with great enthusiasm and are an integral part of Afghan culture, showcasing its traditions and customs. The festivals provide an opportunity for people to come together, celebrate, and strengthen their community ties.
