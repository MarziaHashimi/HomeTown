Foods and Drinks

Afghanistan has a rich culinary tradition, with a variety of dishes and drinks that are unique to the region. One of the most popular dishes is Qabuli Palow, a rice dish made with lamb, carrots, raisins, and spices, which is often served during special occasions and celebrations. Another popular dish is Mantu, which is a type of dumpling filled with spiced ground beef or lamb, served with a tomato-based sauce and topped with yogurt. For drinks, Dogh is a traditional yogurt-based drink that is popular during the summer months, while Chai (tea) is a staple in Afghan culture and is often served with sweets and snacks. Afghan cuisine offers a range of flavors and spices, reflecting the country's diverse cultural influences.
