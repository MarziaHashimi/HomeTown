Sport

Afghanistan has a rich sporting culture, with several traditional sports that are unique to the region. One of the most popular sports in Afghanistan is Buzkashi, which translates to "goat-grabbing." This sport involves horse-mounted players competing to grab a goat carcass and carry it to a scoring area. Buzkashi is a symbol of strength, courage, and masculinity in Afghan culture and has been played for centuries. Other traditional sports in Afghanistan include wrestling, kite-fighting, and falconry. Despite decades of conflict and war, sports continue to play an essential role in Afghan society, promoting physical fitness, unity, and national pride.
